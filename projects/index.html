Our Projects

Blanket distribution at night in the streets of Kolkata

by Helping Hands CC

We celebrated 1st January by giving at least one blanket to someone in need, so they could have dignity. This winter, we committed ourselves to raise resources from our family and friends to make someone's winter a little warmer.

 

 

 
We distributed 100 blankets to the poor people who sleep on the streets of Kolkata, on New Year's night. We would like to specially mention here that everyone donated wholeheartedly when we went to them with our proposal. We are truly very thankful to all who donated and also to all our members, who actively participated in our program. A member of our CC happily offered his car, which eased the process of our distribution. Our work carried on for the whole night, enabling us to give the blankets only to those who were in utmost need of it.

This program taught us: It is not how much we give, but what matters is how much love we put into giving.

 

Inter-relgious awareness programme
 

by Helping Hands CC

We at Helping Hands CC celebrated in the religiously diversified country the World Religion Day on Sunday 21January, 2018. We went to the Birla Mandir, Gurudwara Sri Guru Singh Sabha, St Paul's Cathedral Church, Tipu Sultan Mosque and Jain Swetambara Dadaji Temple, honouring each and every religion.All the religions in the world talk about empathy, giving to the poor, taking responsibility for one's actions, peace and harmony. The conflict between religions arise as we are not well versed about other religions, if we read all the Holy books of different religions we will realize that the essence of every religion is same. We aren't different as we are made to believe by religious fanatics. Early morning Breakfast Distribution at Moulali

by Helping Hands CC

‌"The greatness of humanity is not in being human, but in being humane." - Mahatma Gandhi. ‌ ‌The pavement dwellers on the streets of Kolkata, mostly earn their living by begging. Moulali is a 5 point junction in Entally connecting East Kolkata with Central Kolkata. As it is a busy traffic zone, the moulali junction is the home for around 100+ pavement dwellers.
 

 
 

 
 
‌ ‌As they start begging from around 10 am IST when it's peak office hours, hence most of the time they never get to have breakfast. ‌We, on behalf of Helping Hands CC wanted to provide them with a healthy breakfast consisting of buns, bananas and eggs. ‌ ‌Every weekend, we at Helping Hands CC distributed breakfast at Moulali crossing, Kolkata for around 80 pavement dwellers.
 

 
 

 
 

Tree plantation at saltlake, Kolkata
 

by Helping Hands CC

"He that plants trees, loves others beside himself - Thomas Fuller."
 
 
 
When you plant a tree, you don’t just plant a sapling but hope. We have learned it the hard way that trees are extremely important for our survival. We first resorted to deforestation and made space for more buildings on Earth. Later, when we saw the environment getting seriously affected, we were introduced to something called “afforestation”, a simple process of planting more trees for a better future. In the recent past, with a huge population migrating to urban cities, deforestation took place at an alarming rate to create space leading to major environment issues like air pollution, water pollution, soil pollution and so on.
 

 
 


 
The best way to curb it is by afforestation as tress contribute to the environment by providing oxygen, improving air quality, climate amelioration, conserving water, preserving soil, and supporting wildlife. We started a tree plantation drive in some of the major housing complexes and a few engineering colleges in and around Kolkata, creating an awareness among the residents and students there.Till date we have planted around 700+ trees across Kolkata.
